The Ethical Stack

The fundamental premise of our work is that working for justice and equity in tech starts "at home," within creator communities, and radiates outward from there: to collaborators, adopters, users, all the way down to what we call collateral usersâ€“ the people who are most affected by these technologies.

This is the concept behind what we call the "Ethical Stack"-- a comprehensive strategy layering codes of conduct, transparent governance, explicit social contracts, and ethical licenses.

How We Work

We are proud of our innovative way of working:

  • Our projects are carried out through special-purpose working groups.
  • Members can propose, lead, and participate in working groups according to their interest or expertise.
  • Working groups are volunteer-led, with support from one or more Board sponsors.
  • Working groups can request funds for expert consultations.
  • We work iteratively and collaboratively.
