The Ethical Stack

The fundamental premise of our work is that promoting justice and equity in tech starts "at home," within our open source communities, and radiates outward: to collaborators, adopters, users, all the way down to the people who are directly or indirectly impacted by the technologies we bring into the world.

This is the philosophy behind the Ethical Stack.

The Ethical Stack is a layered approach to bringing ethical considerations to open source. By combining layers, open source communities can choose the tools that best suit their needs and reflect their shared values.

How We Work

We are proud of our innovative way of working:

  • Our projects are carried out through special-purpose working groups.
  • Members can propose, lead, and participate in working groups according to their interest or expertise.
  • Working groups are volunteer-led, with support from one or more Board sponsors.
  • Working groups can request funds for expert consultations.
  • We work ethically, collaboratively, iteratively, and openly.

We center the Ethical Source Principles in all of the work we do.
