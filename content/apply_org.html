+++ title = "Join the Organization for Ethical Source" +++

If you are interested in joining the Organization for Ethical Source as a member, please fill out the form below.

Active Members are individuals who volunteer their time, expertise, and talent towards the work of the organization. Active members have voting rights and do not pay membership fees. All active members will receive an invitation to join the working group's Slack organization.

Supporting Members are individuals or organizations who support the work of the organization through their membership fees. Supporting members do not have voting rights. Annual membership fees are 50€ in the European Union, ¥5,000 in Japan, CHF50 in Switzerland, and $50 in the US and elsewhere.


Thank you for your application. Look for an email from us with confirmation and details on your membership.
