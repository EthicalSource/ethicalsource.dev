Ethical Source Principles

We assure success in adhering to these principles through our impact rather than our intentions.

The practice of Ethical Source is informed by these principles:

  1. Our work benefits the commons.

    2. lalalala lalalalala lalalalala lalalalala

  2. Our work is done in the open.

  4. Our work respects accessibility as a human right.

  5. Our work prioritizes the saftey of people.

    6. lalalala lalalalala lalalalala lalalalala
